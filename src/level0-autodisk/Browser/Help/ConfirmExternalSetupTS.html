Troubleshooting
  1. Is the IR Blaster close enough to your VCR's
    remote control sensor?

  2. Is your VCR correctly connected to your
    WebTV box?

  3. Is your VCR turned on? Is the TV/VCR switch
    set to VCR?

  4. If you're using a VHF connection, did you
    choose the right video output channel?
