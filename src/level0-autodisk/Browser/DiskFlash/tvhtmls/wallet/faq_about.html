More about the &wtv-tvwallet;
With your remote control you can request coupons, information on products, or even
buy things while watching TV.

The &wtv-tvwallet; makes responding to these offers easier because you won't need to type the same info every time. You'll be able to change your &wtv-tvwallet; info at any time through TV Settings. Also, you do not need to fill in your credit card and billing info if you only want to take advantage of free offers. Currently, your &wtv-tvwallet; cannot
be used to shop on the Internet.
