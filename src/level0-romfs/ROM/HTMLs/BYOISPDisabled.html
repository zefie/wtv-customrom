Using your own ISP
If you already have an account with an Internet service provider (ISP), you will soon have the option of using your ISP to connect to WebTV.

When WebTV makes this service available, you'll be able to switch it on by choosing Setup from the home page. You'll need to have your own account with an ISP already.

Of course, you can continue connecting to WebTV as you have all along — through WebTV's national network of points of presence.
